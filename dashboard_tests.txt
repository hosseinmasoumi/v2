Dashboard chart test notes

Experiment charts are split into three groups: embankment, concrete, and asphalt.

Embankment statuses: acceptable, recompact, retest, in progress, under test.
Concrete and asphalt statuses: acceptable, penalty, demolition, in progress, under test.

Volume charts compare accepted executed volume against total planned layer volume for the same work group.

Kilometer validation notes

Upper layer requests are checked only against lower-layer results in the same overlapping kilometer interval.
Km 10.000 to 11.000 data must not affect km 2.000 to 3.000.
